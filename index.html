Jason Robinson's Janus Ensemble, Inc. is a non-profit organization based in Massachusetts led by composer, saxophonist, and flutist Jason Robinson. The organization is dedicated to artistic production and performance, educational outreach, cultural preservation, and community engagement related to the small ensembles and full 17-piece creative music orchestra led by Jason Robinson.
