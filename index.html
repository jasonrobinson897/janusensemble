Jason Robinson's Janus Ensemble, Inc. is the non-profit organization that supports much of the creative work of composer, saxophonist, flutist, and scholar Jason Robinson. Founded in 2009, the ensemble has transformed over the years and now includes small and medium-sized configurations, as well as the flagship 17-piece Janus Orchestra.

Building a Brighter Future With Creative Music

Jason Robinson’s Janus Ensemble, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to the performance, recording, and preservation of visionary creative music. Our work bridges the gap between jazz traditions and experimentalism.

We fulfill our mission through:

  • Artistic Production & Performance: Rehearsal, performance, and recording of original works.
  • Educational Outreach: Workshops and masterclasses teaching the history and techniques of creative music.
  • Cultural Preservation: Commissioning new works and documenting the evolution of the jazz art form.
  • Community Engagement: Increasing accessibility through subsidized public performances.
