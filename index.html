Jason Robinson's Janus Ensemble, Inc. is a non-profit organization that supports the performance, recording, educational outreach, community engagement, and cultural preservation of the composer, saxophonist, and flutist Jason Robinson’s Janus Ensemble. Consisting of a number of small, medium, and large ensemble configurations, including the 17-piece Janus Orchestra, Robinson’s Janus Ensemble is dedicated to commissioning and presenting visionary original music that extends jazz and creative music traditions. Jason Robinson’s Janus Ensemble, Inc partners with community-based and art-related organizations to present live performances and raises funds to provide living wages to musicians and other cultural workers who support live performance.
